Partner with C4AC Labs!

We partner with values-aligned organizations, movements, and initiatives on paid projects through C4AC Labs, C4AC Foundation’s sibling initiative, as a way of sustaining the Foundation beyond traditional grants and funding cycles. Here’s what you can partner with us for!

Communications

From strategy-building to full social media and website management, we help you craft communications that are powerful, intentional, and rooted in your politics.

Tech & Security

We build and maintain accessible, feminist digital infrastructures: website development, security analyses, maintenance, and more, designed with care and sustainability in mind.

Workshops

We provide institutional workshops on creating neurodivergent-affirming workspaces, inclusivity, prevention of sexual harassment at workplace, and more! Our approach to everything we organize go beyond the gender binaries.
