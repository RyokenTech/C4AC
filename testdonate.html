Nu Misra

Social Media Lead

Nu@commsforacause.org

Nu (they/he) is a disabled queer feminist author and the founder of Revival Disability India — a magazine and community building conversations on access intimacy, artvitism and disability justice. They lead digital media for Comms 4 A Cause (C4AC). Nu’s work is rooted in lived experience, storytelling, and the politics of solidarity.

01

A fun fact about you?

I collect disability art zines from around the world — and my suitcase always has more zines than clothes.

02

What is your feminist utopia?

A world where access isn’t done to be “inclusive,” but where access simply is — ordinary, instinctive, and woven into how we live together.

03

Complete the narration, “Safety to me is…”

Being believed, being witnessed, and not having to translate my pain.

04

What does self/community care look like to you?

Co-leading instead of competing. Building together instead of burning out alone.
